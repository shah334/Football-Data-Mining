Roto
Rotisserie, or 'Roto' ranks teams from first to last in each of the league's stat categories. Points are then awarded based on these category rankings and totaled to determine an overall score and league rank.
H2H Points
H2H Points is similar to how Fantasy Football is played. It assigns a point value to individual stat categories (ie. STL=1, etc.) and each matchup's winner is determined solely by which team accumulates the most fantasy points. The end result is a win (1-0-0), loss (0-1-0) or tie (0-0-1). Your team will play a regular season schedule and playoffs to determine the league champion.
H2H Each Category
H2H Each Category has teams competing in each of the league's stat categories, earning a win, loss, or tie in each one. Your team will play a regular season schedule and playoffs to determine the league champion.
H2H Most Categories
H2H Most Categories allows you to select a number of stat categories. For each matchup (usually Monday through Sunday) team totals are accumulated in each of the categories. At the end of the matchup, the winner is determined by which team wins the greater number of categories. The end result is a win (1-0-0), loss (0-1-0) or tie (0-0-1) for each team. Your team will play a regular season schedule and playoffs to determine the league champion.
Season Points
Season Points scoring is similar to H2H points, but instead of points per matchup, points are tallied over the entire. Standings are based on the accumulation of points covering all stat categories and combined into one total points column. The team with the most overall points at the end of the season wins.
