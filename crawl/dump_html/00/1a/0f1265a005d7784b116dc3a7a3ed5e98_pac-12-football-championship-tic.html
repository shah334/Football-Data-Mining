2015 Pac-12 Championship Game to Pit Stanford Against USC

For its second year hosting the Pac-12 Championship game, Levi’s Stadium in Santa Clara, Calif., will feature a never-before-seen finale matchup when the Stanford Cardinal and USC Trojans take to the gridiron on Dec. 5. The 2015 contest will mark Stanford’s third appearance and first since 2013, while USC has qualified for the first time since the game was instituted in 2011. Stanford and USC last met in September when the Cardinal edged the Trojans, 41-31, in Los Angeles. At 10-2, Stanford is still in the running for one of four College Football Playoff spots, though it will likely need a win in the Pac-12 Championship game plus a loss from Michigan State/Iowa, Alabama, or Clemson in their respective conference championship games in order to have a shot at being selected.

History will certainly be on Stanford’s side as the North Division has won every single Pac-12 Championship game thus far and typically by a wide margin. The closest game yet was the Cardinal’s 27-24 victory over UCLA back in 2012.
